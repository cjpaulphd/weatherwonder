Welcome to WeatherWonder!

Your complete weather dashboard — forecast, radar, alerts, and sky data.

Built on free, open weather data from Open-Meteo, RainViewer, and the National Weather Service. No account, no ads, no tracking.

1
Set your location — tap the location name at the top any time to search for a city or ZIP, or share your GPS.
2
Save your places — tap the ☆ beside the location name to favorite it. Favorites live in the ☰ menu for one-tap switching.
3
Dig deeper — every section has an ⓘinfo button explaining how to read it, from radar colors to twilight times.
